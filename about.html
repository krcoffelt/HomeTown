About HomeTown

We're a Kansas City marketing agency on a mission to help local businesses thrive in the digital age.

Rooted in Kansas City. Built for Growth.

We are not a faceless national firm. HomeTown was founded in and for Kansas City. Our mission is to elevate the businesses that make our city unique. We understand the local landscape because we are a part of it.

Our approach is personal. We work with a select number of clients to ensure a deep, collaborative partnership. We're here to be your trusted creative and strategic team, invested in your long-term success.

Our Story

HomeTown was born from a simple observation: Kansas City's incredible small businesses deserved better marketing support. Too many local companies were either priced out of quality marketing services or receiving cookie-cutter solutions from agencies that didn't understand the local market.

Founded in 2020, we set out to bridge this gap. We combined big-agency expertise with small-town values, creating a unique approach that delivers premium results at fair prices. Our team brings together decades of experience from national agencies, tech startups, and creative studios.

Today, we're proud to be Kansas City's trusted marketing partner for businesses that are serious about growth. From the Crossroads to Waldo, from Westport to the River Market, we're helping build the brands that make our city special.
