Pricing FAQs

Are there any setup fees or contracts?

No setup fees, no contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What if I need services not listed here?

We're flexible! Contact us to discuss custom solutions. We can create a package that includes exactly what you need.

Do you offer discounts for paying annually?

Yes! Pay for 12 months upfront and get 2 months free on any monthly plan. We also offer 10% off for referrals and local business partnerships.

What makes your pricing different?

We believe in making marketing accessible to every small business. Our unique options like 'Pay What You Can' and success-based pricing mean you can get started regardless of your current budget.
